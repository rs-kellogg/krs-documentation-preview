📊 WRDS#

WRDS (Wharton Research Data Services) is a web-based data management and research platform providing access to a broad range of financial, economic, and marketing datasets through a unified interface.

Dataset

Brief description

Audit Analytics

Audit information for approximately 20,000 publicly traded firms and 1,500 accounting firms.

Bank Regulatory

Five databases covering regulated depository financial institutions.

Blockholders

Standardized data on blockholders of 1,913 companies.

Bureau van Dijk (Moody’s)

Collective access to three Bureau van Dijk databases covering private and public companies.

CBOE Indexes

VIX and other CBOE indexes measuring market expectations of near-term volatility.

Compustat Global

Financial and stock market data for publicly traded companies in 82 countries.

Compustat North America

Annual and quarterly financial statement data for most publicly held US and Canadian companies.

CRSP

Basic and derived information for securities traded on US exchanges (NYSE, AMEX, and NASDAQ).

Datastream

Extensive historical financial database with global coverage across multiple asset classes.

DealScan (LSTA/LPC)

Primary source for information on the global commercial loan market.

Direct Marketing Educational Foundation (DMEF)

Customer buying histories for approximately 100,000 customers of catalog and non-profit database marketing businesses.

Dow Jones Averages & Total Return Indexes

Dow Jones Composite, Industrial, Transportation, and Utility Averages, plus total return indexes.

ExecuComp

Executive compensation data for publicly traded US companies from Standard & Poor’s.

Fama-French Portfolios and Factors

Benchmark return portfolios and factors based on size and book-to-market equity, for asset pricing research.

Federal Reserve Bank Reports

Two databases collected from Federal Reserve Banks covering banking and monetary data.

FTSE/Russell

Comprehensive coverage of equity and fixed income markets from FTSE/Russell.

I/B/E/S Earnings Estimate History

Summary and individual analyst forecasts of earnings, cash flows, and buy-sell-hold recommendations.

IRI Marketing FactBook

Point-of-sale information from more than 11,300 grocery stores and 7,500 drug stores with continuously tracked UPC-coded purchases.

ISS Directors Data

ISS data on corporate board director characteristics and committee memberships.

ISS Governance Data

ISS governance metrics and ratings for publicly traded companies.

ISS Voting Analytics

Comprehensive data on institutional voting policies, meeting results, and voting patterns.

ISSM Transactions File Database

Tick-by-tick transaction data covering NYSE, AMEX (1983–1992), and NASDAQ (1987–1992).

Mergent FISD

Fixed income securities issue information and pricing from the Mergent FISD database.

MSCI (formerly KLD and GMI Ratings)

Annual ESG performance indicators for publicly traded companies (formerly KLD STATS).

MSRB (Municipal Securities Transaction Data)

Municipal securities transaction data collected and disseminated by the MSRB.

OptionMetrics Ivy DB

End-of-day options quotes, open interest, and trading volume for US exchange-listed equity options.

OTC Markets

Closing quote, trade, and security reference data for OTCQX, OTCQB, and OTC Pink securities.

Philadelphia Stock Exchange (PHLX)

Historical data from the Philadelphia Stock Exchange, the nation’s oldest organized stock exchange.

RavenPack News Analytics

Real-time structured sentiment, relevance, and novelty scores derived from news and text sources.

Refinitiv ESG

One of the industry’s most comprehensive ESG databases, covering over 88% of global market cap.

Research Quotient

Firm-level measure of R&D productivity linking R&D spending to growth and market value.

SDC Platinum

Collection of databases with information on financial transactions including M&A, equity, and debt.

Thomson Institutional (13F) and Mutual Fund (S12) Holdings

Quarterly observations of institutional and mutual fund common stock holdings from 1980 to present.

TRACE

FINRA’s OTC corporate bond market real-time price-dissemination service.

Trade and Quote (TAQ) Database

Intraday trades and quotes for all securities listed on major US exchanges.

Zacks

EPS and revenue estimates, actuals, and stock recommendations from Zacks.

🖥️ Kellogg Linux Cluster (KLC)#

The Kellogg Linux Cluster (KLC) is a high-performance computing environment that hosts proprietary bulk data files available to eligible Kellogg researchers for batch and interactive analysis.

Dataset

Brief description

Arca

Complete limit order book for NYSE Arca ECN trading in listed and OTCBB securities.

BoardEx

Profiles of business executives worldwide including career history, compensation, and board memberships.

CBOT Treasury Package

Tick-by-tick and end-of-day data for all CBOT products (coverage through 2007).

CME

Time and sales and end-of-day data for S&P 500 futures, options, and currency contracts (through 2007).

Comscore

Historical US consumer panel data including desktop URL traffic and ad exposure records (2019–2020).

CORD-19

Open research dataset of COVID-19 and coronavirus literature compiled by the Allen Institute for AI.

Cotality (formerly CoreLogic)

Real property data from county assessor and recorder offices covering over 147 million US properties.

DealScan (LSTA/LPC)

Primary source for information on the global commercial loan market.

EDGAR

All publicly available SEC filings in raw text or HTML format.

First Call Historical Database

First Call real-time earnings estimates and related financial forecast information.

Forrester Research

Annual survey of 40,000+ US and Canadian households analyzing technology behavior patterns.

I/B/E/S Earnings Estimate History

Summary and individual analyst forecasts of earnings, cash flows, and buy-sell-hold recommendations.

Institute for Financial Markets

Daily futures data covering 115 contracts across US, Canadian, and international exchanges.

ISS Securities Class Action Services

Litigation research and claims filing solution covering equities and fixed income across all markets.

ISS Shareholder Proposals

ISS data on shareholder proposal submissions, outcomes, and voting records.

ISSM Transactions File Database

Tick-by-tick transaction data covering NYSE, AMEX (1983–1992), and NASDAQ (1987–1992).

Lipper Hedge Fund

Quantitative performance data on over 6,300 active hedge funds and over 7,000 graveyard funds.

NAIC Financial Statement Data

Financial statement data filed by insurance companies with the NAIC, covering 2001–2017.

NAIC Property/Casualty Insurance Data

NAIC Property/Casualty InfoPro annual data for 1993–2004.

Nielsen Marketing Data

Three marketing datasets (Homescan, Retail Scanner, and Ad Intel) from Kellogg’s Nielsen subscription.

PATSTAT

Worldwide patent database compiled by the European Patent Office.

ProxyCurl

Snapshot of approximately 170 million LinkedIn profiles.

R&C Futures Data

End-of-day and intraday historical futures data covering 80 global commodities.

Value Line

Value Line analysts’ estimates and 3-to-5-year projections for approximately 1,700 companies.

Zacks

EPS and revenue estimates, actuals, and stock recommendations from Zacks.

☁️ Redivis#

Redivis is a cloud-based data platform operated by Northwestern’s Kellogg Research Support and University Libraries that lets researchers discover, query, and analyze datasets directly in their browser.

Dataset

Brief description

Numerator

Longitudinal individual-level consumer purchase data from a large US panel, including transaction histories, product detail, and household demographics.

L2 VM2 Uniform

National voter file with ~200M records covering registration, voting history (2000–present), demographics, and consumer data for all 50 states.

ProxyCurl

Snapshot of approximately 170 million LinkedIn profiles.

🗄️ Kellogg Data Cloud (KDC)#

The Kellogg Data Cloud (KDC) is an Amazon Athena-based platform that hosts Kellogg-licensed datasets for SQL-based query and analysis.

Dataset

Brief description

Census Data

Demographic, economic, and geographic data from the US Census Bureau.

Comscore

Historical US consumer panel data including desktop URL traffic and ad exposure records (2019–2020).

Cotality (formerly CoreLogic)

Real property data from county assessor and recorder offices covering over 147 million US properties.

PATSTAT

Worldwide patent database compiled by the European Patent Office.

Taxi

New York City taxi trip records obtained via FOIL request from the NYC Taxi & Limousine Commission.

Web of Science

Multidisciplinary coverage of over 10,000 journals in sciences, social sciences, arts, and humanities.

📚 Northwestern Library#

Northwestern University Libraries provides access to a range of licensed databases and data services available to the Northwestern research community.

Dataset

Brief description

Bureau van Dijk (Moody’s)

Collective access to three Bureau van Dijk databases covering private and public companies.

IMF International Financial Statistics

IMF time series covering more than 200 countries across approximately 32,000 economic indicators.

Mergent Investext

SEC filings, company annual reports, and analyst research reports from Mergent Investext.

Web of Science

Multidisciplinary coverage of over 10,000 journals in sciences, social sciences, arts, and humanities.
