## 📊 WRDS

[WRDS (Wharton Research Data Services)](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/) is a web-based data management and research platform providing access to a broad range of financial, economic, and marketing datasets through a unified interface.

| Dataset | Brief description |
|---|---|
| {doc}`datasets/audit-analytics` | Audit information for approximately 20,000 publicly traded firms and 1,500 accounting firms. |

| {doc}`datasets/cboe-indexes` | VIX and other CBOE indexes measuring market expectations of near-term volatility. |
| {doc}`datasets/compustat-global` | Financial and stock market data for publicly traded companies in 82 countries. |
| {doc}`datasets/compustat-north-america` | Annual and quarterly financial statement data for most publicly held US and Canadian companies. |
| {doc}`datasets/crsp` | Basic and derived information for securities traded on US exchanges (NYSE, AMEX, and NASDAQ). |
| {doc}`datasets/datastream` | Extensive historical financial database with global coverage across multiple asset classes. |
| {doc}`datasets/dealscan--lsta-lpc-` | Primary source for information on the global commercial loan market. |

| {doc}`datasets/comscore` | Historical US consumer panel data including desktop URL traffic and ad exposure records (2019–2020). |
| {doc}`datasets/cord-19` | Open research dataset of COVID-19 and coronavirus literature compiled by the Allen Institute for AI. |
| {doc}`datasets/cotality` | Real property data from county assessor and recorder offices covering over 147 million US properties. |
| {doc}`datasets/dealscan--lsta-lpc-` | Primary source for information on the global commercial loan market. |
| {doc}`datasets/edgar` | All publicly available SEC filings in raw text or HTML format. |
| {doc}`datasets/first-call-historical-database` | First Call real-time earnings estimates and related financial forecast information. |
| {doc}`datasets/forrester-research` | Annual survey of 40,000+ US and Canadian households analyzing technology behavior patterns. |
| {doc}`datasets/i-b-e-s-earnings-estimate-history` | Summary and individual analyst forecasts of earnings, cash flows, and buy-sell-hold recommendations. |
| {doc}`datasets/institute-for-financial-markets` | Daily futures data covering 115 contracts across US, Canadian, and international exchanges. |
| {doc}`datasets/iss-securities-class-action-services` | Litigation research and claims filing solution covering equities and fixed income across all markets. |

| {doc}`datasets/lipper-hedge-fund` | Quantitative performance data on over 6,300 active hedge funds and over 7,000 graveyard funds. |
| {doc}`datasets/naic-financial-statement-data` | Financial statement data filed by insurance companies with the NAIC, covering 2001–2017. |
| {doc}`datasets/naic-property-casualty-insurance-data` | NAIC Property/Casualty InfoPro annual data for 1993–2004. |
| {doc}`datasets/nielsen-marketing-data` | Three marketing datasets (Homescan, Retail Scanner, and Ad Intel) from Kellogg's Nielsen subscription. |
| {doc}`datasets/patstat` | Worldwide patent database compiled by the European Patent Office. |

| {doc}`datasets/r&c-futures-data` | End-of-day and intraday historical futures data covering 80 global commodities. |

## ☁️ Redivis

[Redivis](https://redivis.com) is a cloud-based data platform operated by Northwestern's Kellogg Research Support and University Libraries that lets researchers discover, query, and analyze datasets directly in their browser.

| Dataset | Brief description |
|---|---|
| {doc}`datasets/numerator` | Longitudinal individual-level consumer purchase data from a large US panel, including transaction histories, product detail, and household demographics. |
| {doc}`datasets/l2-vm2-uniform` | National voter file with ~200M records covering registration, voting history (2000–present), demographics, and consumer data for all 50 states. |
| {doc}`datasets/proxycurl` | Snapshot of approximately 170 million LinkedIn profiles. |

## 🗄️ Kellogg Data Cloud (KDC)

The {doc}`Kellogg Data Cloud (KDC) <../../services/kellogg-data-cloud/kdc>` is an Amazon Athena-based platform that hosts Kellogg-licensed datasets for SQL-based query and analysis.

| Dataset | Brief description |
|---|---|
| {doc}`datasets/census-data` | Demographic, economic, and geographic data from the US Census Bureau. |
| {doc}`datasets/comscore` | Historical US consumer panel data including desktop URL traffic and ad exposure records (2019–2020). |
| {doc}`datasets/cotality` | Real property data from county assessor and recorder offices covering over 147 million US properties. |
| {doc}`datasets/patstat` | Worldwide patent database compiled by the European Patent Office. |
| {doc}`datasets/taxi` | New York City taxi trip records obtained via FOIL request from the NYC Taxi & Limousine Commission. |
| {doc}`datasets/web-of-science` | Multidisciplinary coverage of over 10,000 journals in sciences, social sciences, arts, and humanities. |
