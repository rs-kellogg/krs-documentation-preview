## Description

The OTC Markets End-of-Day (EOD) Pricing dataset, available on the Wharton Research Data Services (WRDS) platform, provides comprehensive daily pricing and market activity data for securities traded on over-the-counter (OTC) markets in the United States. This dataset is sourced from OTC Markets Group, the operator of the largest electronic quotation and trading platform for OTC securities.

The OTC market consists of securities that are not listed on major national exchanges (such as NYSE or NASDAQ); instead, these securities are traded through a decentralized dealer network. The OTC market is an important segment of the U.S. equity market, including:

- Smaller domestic companies that do not meet exchange listing requirements
- Foreign companies that prefer not to list on U.S. exchanges but wish to maintain U.S. market access
- American Depository Receipts (ADRs) for international securities
- Penny stocks and early-stage companies
- Securities that have been delisted from major exchanges

The EOD Pricing database captures end-of-day quotes, trade prices, trading volumes, and other security-level information, making it a valuable resource for academic research on market microstructure, price discovery in less liquid markets, information asymmetry, and the behavior of smaller or less regulated securities.

## Access

This dataset is available via [Wharton Research Data Services (WRDS)](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/). WRDS offers both a web interface for small interactive queries and the WRDS Cloud for running batch programs. Faculty may request a temporary class account for course use.
