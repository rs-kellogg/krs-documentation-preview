## Description

Daily futures data from the [Institute for Financial Markets](http://www.theifm.org) cover 115 different contracts across US, Canadian, and non-North American exchanges. Data include prices (open, high, low, and settlement), volume, and open interest. The availability and time coverage of individual variables depends on the contract. The period covered (in terms of contract end dates) ranges from 1969 (contracts made in 1968) through 2001 (contracts made through September 1999).

## Access

Data are stored at `/kellogg/data/futures` on the Kellogg Linux Cluster (KLC) and are available to current Kellogg faculty and doctoral students.
