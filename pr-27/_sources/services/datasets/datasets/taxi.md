## Description

This dataset was obtained through a Freedom of Information Law (FOIL) request from the New York City Taxi & Limousine Commission (NYCT&L). It covers 2013 taxi operations in New York City.

## Access

Taxi files are available to current Kellogg faculty and doctoral students for use in academic research. The data are available on the [Kellogg Data Cloud (KDC)](/services/kellogg-data-cloud/kdc.md).
