## Description

Data updates ended in 2007. Selective CME time and sales, and end-of-day data files, which include data on S&P 500 futures, options, derivatives, British pound, and Japanese Yen. See full list of [CME Files](https://nuwildcat.sharepoint.com/:b:/s/KSM-RSDataDocumentation/EdZTYx8_w19CjKUCgoRxejwB5Sx3otmjBS0QTwkp1Kfq5g?e=bqfQiN).

## Access

CME files are available to current Kellogg faculty and doctoral students for use in academic research on the [Kellogg Linux Cluster (KLC)](http://www.kellogg.northwestern.edu/rs/computing/kellogg-linux-cluster.aspx).

Additional information on Futures Contract Symbols can be found on the [Moore Research Center, Inc. (MRCI)](https://www.mrci.com/web/online-explanation-pages/symbols-a-codes.html) website.
