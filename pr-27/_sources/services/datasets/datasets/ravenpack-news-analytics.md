## Description

Using over 40,000 sources, RavenPack provides real-time news analytics, including sentiment analysis and event data focused on business and financial applications. Data includes news and social media content, allowing for comprehensive analysis of financial markets.

Researchers use RavenPack to study how news affects markets — examining stock return predictability, volatility dynamics, and event-driven price reactions. It is also widely used for constructing sentiment-based factors in asset pricing, enhancing risk and stress models, and analyzing the transmission of macro or geopolitical news across asset classes.
