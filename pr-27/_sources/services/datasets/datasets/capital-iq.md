## Description

Capital IQ is a web-based application that provides access to corporate information and financials for public and private companies, financial transactions, and professionals. Coverage is restricted to active companies, with data series going back to approximately 1992. The platform also includes some macroeconomic data.

## Access

Capital IQ is only available to authorized members of the Kellogg School of Management. A VPN connection is required when accessing Capital IQ from outside the Evanston or Miami campuses. SSO is not available for this resource.

Access Capital IQ by visiting [https://web.capitaliq.com](https://www.capitaliq.com/CIQDotNet/Login-okta.aspx) and logging in with your individual account credentials. First-time users must register a new account using their `@kellogg.northwestern.edu` email address.
