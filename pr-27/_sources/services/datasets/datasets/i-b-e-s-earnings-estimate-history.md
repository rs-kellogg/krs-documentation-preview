# I/B/E/S Earnings Estimate History

## At a Glance

Find information [here](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/pages/get-data/lseg-ibes/).

If you have questions, reach out to [rs@kellogg.northwestern.edu](mailto:rs@kellogg.northwestern.edu).

## Description

IBES provides summary and individual analyst forecasts of company earnings, cash flows, and other important financial items, as well as buy-sell-hold recommendations.

There are two versions of the IBES earnings estimate history:

- **Summary History:** A snapshot of consensus-level data taken monthly. The US edition covers reported earnings estimates and results since January 1976; the international edition starts in 1987.
- **Detail History (Analyst Earnings Estimate History):** A timeline of individual analysts' earnings forecasts (daily records at the analyst level). The US edition starts in 1983; the international edition starts in 1987.

Both datasets are available for US and international stocks, covering 52 markets.

## Access

This dataset is available via [Wharton Research Data Services (WRDS)](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/), under the [LSEG IBES](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/pages/get-data/lseg-ibes/) subscription. WRDS offers both a web interface for small interactive queries and the WRDS Cloud for running batch programs. Faculty may request a temporary class account for course use.

Historic data is also available on the [Kellogg Linux Cluster (KLC)](http://www.kellogg.northwestern.edu/rs/computing/kellogg-linux-cluster.aspx) in the directory `/kellogg/data/ibes_intl`.
