## Description

The U.S. Census Bureau is America's leading source of quality data about its people, businesses, and economy. The Bureau produces a wide range of demographic, economic, and geographic data products.

## Access

Demographic, economic, geographic, and other data are available directly on the [Census website](https://www.census.gov/data/data-tools.html).

Research Support hosts some Census data locally on the [Kellogg Data Center (KDC)](https://www.kellogg.northwestern.edu/rs/computing/kellogg-data-center.aspx). To inquire about locally hosted Census data, contact [Research Support](mailto:rs@kellogg.northwestern.edu).
