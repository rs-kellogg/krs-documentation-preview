## Description

RCA (Real Capital Analytics) provides a database of commercial property transactions — $10 million or greater globally and $2.5 million or greater in the US — covering office, industrial, retail, multifamily, hotel, and development sites.

## Access

Access the data by logging in to the [RCA (Real Capital Analytics)](https://www.rcanalytics.com) external site. First-time users must request account creation by emailing [real-estate@kellogg.northwestern.edu](mailto:real-estate@kellogg.northwestern.edu). VPN may be required for off-campus access.
