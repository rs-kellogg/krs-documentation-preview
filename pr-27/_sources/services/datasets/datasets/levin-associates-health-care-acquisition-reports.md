## Description

Levin Associates' annual Health Care Services Acquisition Reports provide data on mergers and acquisitions across different sectors in the health care industry, including:

- Hospitals
- Managed care companies
- Physician medical groups
- Rehabilitation
- Labs, MRI, and dialysis
- Behavioral healthcare

Years available: 2007–2018.

## Access

Current Northwestern faculty and doctoral students wishing to use this data should contact Research Support at [rs@kellogg.northwestern.edu](mailto:rs@kellogg.northwestern.edu).
