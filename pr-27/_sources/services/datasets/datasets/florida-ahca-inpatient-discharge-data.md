## Description

The Agency for Health Care Administration collects inpatient discharge, outpatient and ambulatory, and emergency room data from acute, short-term psychiatric, long-term psychiatric, and comprehensive rehabilitation hospitals in Florida. The data are updated on a quarterly basis, and there are approximately 2.5 million records per year. Each record corresponds to an individual inpatient hospital, outpatient, or ER visit. The file contains patient-level data including:

- Patient demographics
- Diagnosis and procedure codes
- Attending physician
- Operating or performing physicians
- Total gross charges
