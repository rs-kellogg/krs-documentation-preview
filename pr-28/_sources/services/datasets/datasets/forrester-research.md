## Description

The North American Consumer Technographics Survey & Data is an annual survey of more than 40,000 US and Canadian households with questions that analyze patterns in technology behavior among consumers in the US and Canada. Kellogg has purchased data from waves 1998 through 2010.

While the survey instrument focuses mostly on the online behaviors of connected consumers, it also includes questions about general technology attitudes and standard demographic and psychographic questions. Topic areas include (but are not limited to):

- Automotive
- Consumer technology
- Financial services
- Healthcare
- Marketing
- Media
- Retail
- Travel

All original-format files (SPSS `.sav` data files) are included in a ZIP file. All files have been translated to Stata format and renamed in a consistent format (`1998BM.dta` through `2010BM.dta`, one year of data per file).

## Access

The files are available on the [Kellogg Linux Cluster (KLC)](https://www.kellogg.northwestern.edu/rs/computing/kellogg-linux-cluster.aspx) in the directory `/kellogg/data/forrester`.

**Citation:** The general format for a citation of Forrester Research data is "Title, Forrester Research, Inc., Date." In particular, Forrester Research suggests: *North American Consumer Technographics Survey & Data, Forrester Research, Inc, September 2010.*

Forrester datasets are not to be shared with individuals or groups outside of the Kellogg School of Management, with the exception of PhD students and RAs working under the supervision of a Northwestern University professor. All users must adhere to [Forrester's citation and fair use policies](https://www.forrester.com/policies/citations-policy/).
