## Description

EDGAR Online offers company data and public filings for equities, mutual funds, and other publicly traded assets going back to 1994. Available data includes:

- Fundamentals
- SEC Filings
- Institutional Ownership
- Insider Trade Data
- IPO/SPO
- E-Prospectus
- Money Market Data
- Quotes and Charts
