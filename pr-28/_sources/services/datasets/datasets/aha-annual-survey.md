## Description

The AHA Annual Survey Database is a comprehensive census of United States hospitals based on the AHA Annual Survey of Hospitals. The database is a reliable resource for health service research and trends analyses, and it offers a snapshot of hospital-specific data on approximately 6,500 hospitals and 400-plus systems, including as many as 1,000 data fields covering:

- Organizational structure
- Personnel
- Hospital facilities and services
- Financial performance

It includes a documentation book with a summary of changes that identifies open, closed, and merged hospitals compared to the prior year. The database is released annually in October. Available at Northwestern: 1990–2012 (even numbered years).
