# Direct Marketing Educational Foundation (DMEF)

## At a Glance

Find information [here](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/pages/get-data/direct-marketing-educational-foundation-dmef/).

If you have questions, reach out to [rs@kellogg.northwestern.edu](mailto:rs@kellogg.northwestern.edu).

## Description

Four individual data sets, each containing customer buying history for approximately 100,000 customers of nationally known catalog and non-profit database marketing businesses.

Corporate names are anonymous and customer names and addresses have been removed, but the business type is indicated. ZIP codes have been retained (if possible) to provide a potential link to Census ZIP-level demographics.

The Direct Marketing Educational Foundation (DMEF) has been incorporated into the Association of National Advertisers (ANA). You may be able to find some information on their [website](https://www.ana.net/content/show?id=thedmaorg-redirect)

## Access

This dataset is available via [Wharton Research Data Services (WRDS)](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/). WRDS offers both a web interface for small interactive queries and the WRDS Cloud for running batch programs. Faculty may request a temporary class account for course use.
