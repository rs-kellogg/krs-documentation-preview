## Description

The Economatica database covers equity performance and financial fundamentals for over 5,000 companies in Latin American countries, including Argentina, Brazil, Chile, Colombia, Mexico, Peru, and Venezuela, as well as equities traded on NASDAQ and NYSE. Economatica also includes over 400 US-company results for use as benchmarks.

Until January 2003, the database also covered the following Asian countries: China, Hong Kong, South Korea, and Taiwan; this data (through Q3 2002) remains available.

Data starts as early as 1986, depending on the country.

## Access

Access to Economatica is available by downloading the [Excel Add-In for Economatica](http://confluence.economatica.com/display/FE/Add-in+Excel+for+Economatica). The "Economatica" tab in Excel contains a "Login" button. Log in using your `@kellogg` email address.

Access is restricted to computers connected to the Northwestern University network. When working off campus, connect to the Northwestern VPN first.

You can also log in to the [legacy Economatica application](https://economatica.com/latam/). As with the Excel add-in, you must be on the Northwestern network and log in using your `@kellogg` email address.
