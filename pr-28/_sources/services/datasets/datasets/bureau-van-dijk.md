# Bureau van Dijk (Moody's)

## At a Glance

Find information [here](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/pages/about/data-vendors/bureau-van-dijk-bvd/).

If you have questions, reach out to [rs@kellogg.northwestern.edu](mailto:rs@kellogg.northwestern.edu).

## Description

Kellogg provides collective access to three Bureau van Dijk databases:

**AMADEUS:** Amadeus from Bureau Van Dijk contains financials, stock prices, ownership, and subsidiaries information for approximately 19 million public and private companies in 34 European countries.

**ORBIS:** Orbis contains information on as many companies as possible, across all countries, and focuses on private companies. Moody's sources the information from over 170 different providers and standardized formats to accommodate regional variations in filing regulations and accountancy practices.

Orbis contains information on approximately 650 million private and public companies and entities across more than 200 countries and territories. Of these, 54 million companies have detailed financial information, and more than 99% are private companies.

**OSIRIS:** Osiris has information on listed, and major unlisted/delisted, companies around the world. The information is very detailed and includes a lot more than financial reports. Different templates are used to show accounts in the correct formats for their company type and location. It covers around 80,000 companies.

## Access

These datasets are available via [Wharton Research Data Services (WRDS)](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/). WRDS offers both a web interface for small interactive queries and the WRDS Cloud for running batch programs. Faculty may request a temporary class account for course use.

The Northwestern Library also provides access to the [ORBIS web interface](http://libguides.northwestern.edu/go.php?c=4023376), which may include items not available in the WRDS files and covers data up to the present (as opposed to the end of the last calendar year on WRDS).

## Data Coverage & Key Identifiers

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| **Time period** | Varies by product (up to 20 years historical) |
| **Geographic coverage** | Global (41+ countries for AMADEUS; 190+ countries for OSIRIS; worldwide for ORBIS) |
| **Unit of observation** | Company-year |
| **Primary identifier** | National identification number (varies by country) |
| **Commonly linked via** | TBD |
| **Key variables** | Balance sheet items, profit and loss items, financial ratios, company descriptors, executive information, industry codes |
