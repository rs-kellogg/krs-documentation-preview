## Description

ISS (formerly MSCI IRRC and RiskMetrics) collects information on year-end outstanding grants, weighted average exercise price of options outstanding, and weighted average contractual life of outstanding options, as well as information on new grants and option exercises. The data includes all options granted, collected from 10K and proxy disclosures, for firms in the S&P 500, the S&P Mid Cap 400, and the S&P SmallCap 600 indices. Kellogg purchased four years of data between 1997 and 2004 (waves: 1997, 2000, 2003, 2004). In principle, options granted for the missing years (1998, 1999, 2001, and 2002) can be reconstructed with the lagged information provided in the files.

## Access

Access is restricted to regular Kellogg faculty and doctoral students only. Visiting faculty and visiting scholars are not authorized to use these data. Do not copy and share these data with anyone. The school's contract with IRRC restricts access to five concurrent users.
