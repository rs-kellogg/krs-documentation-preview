## Description

Lipper Hedge Fund Database (formerly Lipper/TASS) offers quantitative performance data on over 6,300 actively reporting hedge funds and funds of hedge funds, plus over 7,000 graveyard funds that have closed, been liquidated, or stopped reporting. Each module provides tables organized by data themes:

- **Product Details:** Essential fund-profile data including fund strategy, inception date, fund domicile, and more
- **Performance:** Full historical monthly price and performance, dating to fund inception
- **Notes:** Manager biographies, fund structure, fees, leverage, and more
- **Focus Details:** Strategy trends grouped by Investment, Sector, or Geographical Focus, or Investment Approach
- **Contacts:** Contact information for each manager and listings of service providers
- Additional tables include Indices, Stock and Trading Exchanges, and more

## Access

Lipper data is available on the Kellogg Linux Cluster (KLC) under `/kellogg/data/lipper`.
