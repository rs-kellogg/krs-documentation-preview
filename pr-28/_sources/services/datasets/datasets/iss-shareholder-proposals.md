## Description

ISS Shareholder Proposals is part of the ISS product suite (formerly published by IRRC, part of the RiskMetrics product by MSCI). ISS provides research on corporate governance, proxy voting, and corporate responsibility issues.

The database tracks shareholder resolutions related to corporate governance (but not social policy), covering approximately 5,000 companies annually. For each resolution it records:

- Sponsor of the proposal
- Issue or topic
- Company name
- Meeting date
- Votes for and against
- Outcome

Kellogg acquired two purchases of this data: one in 2006 (covering 1997–2005) and one in mid-2012 (covering 2006–2011).

**Note on ownership changes:** Due to changes in IRRC's ownership structure (acquired by ISS and later renamed "RiskMetrics"), the data changed in 2011. The codes used to track different shareholder proposals differ between the pre-2011 and 2011 data.

**Note on historical data:** There is some shareholder proposal data from 1972 through 1996 that Kellogg has not purchased. That earlier data is very sparse — for 1973, for example, only 40 shareholder proposals are recorded.

The licensing agreement between Kellogg and RiskMetrics (IRRC) allows access only to regular Kellogg faculty (no visitors) and Kellogg doctoral students. Do not copy and share these data with anyone.

## Access
