## Description

MSCI ESG KLD STATS is an annual dataset of positive and negative environmental, social, and governance (ESG) performance indicators applied to a universe of publicly traded companies. The dataset was created by KLD Research & Analytics in 1991; MSCI acquired KLD in 2010.

The corporate governance component of this dataset (formerly GMI Ratings) is updated annually and contains approximately 225 unique data points organized into four primary categories for approximately 3,000 US companies:

- Companies
- Directors
- CEO Compensation
- Takeover Defenses

Primary data categories covered include:

- Corporate Board Structure
- Independent Director Positions
- Committee Assignments
- CEO Compensation
- Director Compensation
- Audit Fees
- Takeover Defenses
- Corporate Ownership

## Access
