## Description

Kellogg has acquired a license for the 1993–2004 NAIC Property/Casualty InfoPro annual data. These data provide detailed financial information on almost all property-casualty insurers doing business in the United States during that period. The data cover:

- Premiums (revenues), costs, and equity
- Reported development of claims of different types over time
- Allocation of assets across investment types

Since electronic filing has become more widespread, the completeness of the data increases in the more recent years.

## Access

The NAIC Property/Casualty InfoPro data are available to all current Kellogg faculty and doctoral students on the [Kellogg Linux Cluster](https://www.kellogg.northwestern.edu/rs/computing/kellogg-linux-cluster.aspx) under `/kellogg/data/naic`. Files are in comma-separated-value ASCII format.

All files are comma-delimited without headers. The naming convention is "Pyyyy###," where "yyyy" is the year (1993–2004) and "###" is a number that identifies the type of file. For example, "P1993000.csv" contains the demographic data for the reporting companies in 1993.

The files reproduce their printed yearbook almost page by page.
