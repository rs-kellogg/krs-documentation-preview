## Description

The Global Financial Database is available to current Northwestern faculty, staff, and students for academic research.

The GFD includes time series, some starting in 1265, for a variety of indicators and about 200 markets in the world:

- Returns for different asset classes
- Commodity prices
- Macroeconomic data
- Stock market indices
- Exchange rates
- Interbank rates
- Swap rates
- Treasury-bill yields
- Long-term government bond yields
- Development database (population, defense and conflict, education, energy production, environment, etc.)

## Access

Request an account using your Kellogg email address on the [GFD Platform](http://turing.library.northwestern.edu/login?url=https://finaeon.globalfinancialdata.com). Accounts cannot be shared.

Once your account is approved, you can [log in here](https://www-globalfinancialdata-com.turing.library.northwestern.edu/gfdplatform/account/login).
