## Description

The Equifax Analytic dataset provides information such as credit risk scores, consumer age range, geography, debt balances, and delinquency status at the loan level for a ten percent sample of the U.S. credit-active population, with historic data starting in 2005.

## Access

To receive access, you must be directly affiliated with the Guthrie Center for Real Estate Research. Please contact Professor Efraim Benmelech, Director of the Guthrie Center, to request access.
