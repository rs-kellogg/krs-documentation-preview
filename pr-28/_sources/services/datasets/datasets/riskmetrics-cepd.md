## Description

RiskMetrics Corporate Environmental Profiles Database (CEPD) contains environmental compliance data for 2,065 publicly traded companies, including the entire S&P 1500. The majority of companies are US-incorporated (1,972 of 2,065). Data cover 19 environmental statutes:

- Atomic Energy Act
- Clean Air Act
- Clean Water Act
- Emergency Response Notification System Chemical Spills
- Emergency Response Notification System Oil Spills
- Endangered Species Act
- Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act
- Minerals Management Service
- Mining Safety and Health Act
- Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA) Corrective Actions
- RCRA Permit Denials
- Safe Drinking Water Act
- Superfund: number of National Priority List (NPL) sites where the company has been identified as a Potentially Responsible Party by the EPA
- Toxics Release Inventory (TRI): emissions and off-site transfers for disposal of listed chemicals
- TRI One-Time Releases: total quantity of toxic chemical released due to non-routine production events
- TRI Dioxin: emissions and off-site transfers of dioxin
- TRI Energy Recovery and Recycling: on-site and off-site energy recovery and recycling of listed chemicals
- TRI Treated Toxic Chemicals: amount of toxic chemical treated on-site or sent off-site for treatment
- Toxic Substances Control Act

The Company Universe table and the CEPD Totals table present the data in its most basic form.

## Access

These data are available to Kellogg faculty and doctoral students only. The CEPD consists of three files:

- [CEPD data dictionary](https://nuwildcat.sharepoint.com/:w:/s/KSM-RSDataDocumentation/EZfEmGkgJ7hJs_t91g3w_y8BsaH-0CYHDxhThnBAAa01XA?e=HdEeRz) — data and methods for the CEPD online service and custom datafeeds
- [Company Universe](http://www.kellogg.northwestern.edu/rc/sec/faculty_and_phd-only/riskmetrics/CEPDandIVA/COMPANY_UNIVERSE.zip) — ZIP file containing one Excel spreadsheet with a list of companies and their identifiers, including an IRRC company ID
- [Totals](http://www.kellogg.northwestern.edu/rc/sec/faculty_and_phd-only/riskmetrics/CEPDandIVA/TOTALS.zip) — ZIP file containing the panel CEPD data, presenting all CEPD indicators from 1996 through 2006 by IRRC company ID
