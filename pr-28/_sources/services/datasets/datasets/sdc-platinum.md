## Description

SDC Platinum is a collection of databases with information on financial transactions. Kellogg subscribes to the following sub-databases:

- **U.S. and Non-U.S. Mergers & Acquisitions:** U.S. targets (1979 to present); Non-U.S. targets (1985 to present); Joint ventures/alliances (1988 to present); Repurchases (1994 to present).
- **Global New Issues:** Domestic and international new issues — U.S. IPOs, Asia-Pacific domestic, Australian and New Zealand domestic, Continental European domestic, Indian domestic, Japanese domestic, Latin American domestic, United Kingdom domestic, and Rest of World domestic. Also includes Medium-term Notes Programs and Underwritten Calls.
- **Venture Capital (VentureXpert):** Industry resources (funds, firms, portfolio companies, individuals); industry statistics including fund commitments, performance, and statistics; company investment/disbursement, IPOs, and M&A. Most databases have data from 1970 to present; some start in 1969.
- **Corporate Restructuring:** Full-detail bankruptcies (1988 to present); limited-detail bankruptcies (1980 through 1990).

## Access

> This dataset is available via [Wharton Research Data Services (WRDS)](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/). WRDS offers both a web interface for small interactive queries and the WRDS Cloud for running batch programs. Faculty may request a temporary class account for course use.

**Note:** The SDC Platinum stand-alone desktop application was discontinued in January 2024. Kellogg's SDC subscriptions are now available exclusively through WRDS.

## Data Coverage & Key Identifiers

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| **Time period** | Varies by sub-database; M&A from 1979 (US) / 1985 (Non-US); New issues from ~1970; VentureXpert from ~1969 |
| **Geographic coverage** | Global |
| **Unit of observation** | Transaction (deal, issuance, or filing) |
| **Key variables** | Deal value, announcement date, completion date, acquirer/target identifiers, deal type, transaction status |
